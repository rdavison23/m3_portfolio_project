About

I was born in Ethiopia and moved to the U.S. when I was 11, bringing with me a love for creativity and craftsmanship. As an apparel designer, I have a sharp eye for color and textiles, plus solid experience in garment construction, pattern making, and styling. Now, I am diving into the digital world, learning to code with JavaScript and HTML. Blending fashion and technology opens up exciting new possibilities whether through interactive web experiences, e-commerce, or digital fashion projects. I am always eager to learn, push boundaries, and take on challenges with fresh ideas and determination.
